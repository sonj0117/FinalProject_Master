Namsan Seoul Tower

Namsan tower is one of the most iconic landmarks in Seoul, where you can enjoy the 360-dgree views of the entire Seoul city. Three ways to go up the observatory are through cable cars, hike, and the shuttle buses. Make sure to head over to the tower observatory to enjoy the the full 360-degree views. One must do activities for couples or friends is the love lock in the observatory.You can write a message on your locks and lock them and throw the keys away. These locks are meant for eternal love and friendship! You can easily buy the keys so make sure to check it out!

Opearting Hours Tues to Sun: 9AM-11PM

Lotte World Tower

Lotte World Tower, which is the fifth tallest building in the world is full of things to do! You can enjoy the mesmerizing view of seoul in the Skydeck or a speaksy bar named, BAR 81, located on the 81st floor of the building. Moreover, it is connected to Lotte World Mall, which provides wide selections of brands to shop and restaurants to eat! They even have a movie theater in the mall. It is a perfect place to go if you want to do different things in one place!

Skydeck Opearting Hours: Sun to Thurs: 10AM-10PM Fri to Sat: 10AM-11PM
Lottel World Mall Operating Hours: Mon to Sun: 10AM-8PM
Bar 81 Operating Hours: Sun-Thurs: 6PM-1AM Fri-Sat: 6PM-2AM

Leeum, Samsung Museum of Art

Leeum Museum is the leading art museum in Seoul. They offer collections of both traditional Korean art and contempoary art. The musuem in divided into two parts: traditional art exhibition and modern art exhibition. Interior of the traditional art exhibition building resembles the spiral ramp gallery of the Guggenheim museum in New York City.

Opearting Hours Tues-Sum: 10:30AM-6:00PM
