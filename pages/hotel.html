I. Shilla Hotel Seoul

Shilla hotel has been one of Seoul’s top hotels since its opening in 1979. Shilla is a kingdom name during a period when Korea was split into three different kingdoms centuries ago. The name showcases well the balance of modern service and traditional architecture such as Youngbin Hall. Its prime location and excellent service make it the optimal place for tourists, but Seoul residents often come to enjoy time at the swimming pool, the spa, and most of all the restaurants. Even if you’re not planning to stay at the Shilla Hotel, enjoy a meal at its most famous buffet.

II. Park Hyatt Hotel

Park Hyatt prizes its prime location, in Samsung-dong, walking distance to Coex and Hyundai Department Store. The spacious rooms have a modern feel with ceiling to floor windows, chic wood, and white interior. The hotel is also very famous for its rooftop floor and its infinity pool. At night experience a relaxing swim while viewing Seoul’s gorgeous night view full of lights and skyscrapers. If you plan to visit during the summer, don’t forget to order their Mango Bingsu, traditional shaved ice dessert.

III. L'Escape Hotel

If you are looking for a Parisian escape in the midst of Seoul, your best option would be L’escape Hotel. L’escape is a five star located in the middle of Myungdong area, which is one of the most popular tourist locations. The second you step into the doors of L’escape, you feel like you are the princess or a prince of 19th century France. The antique interiors with intricate patterns will provide an unforgettable experience! Each room is designed differently, which makes the whole experience better.
