What is your favorite Cousine?

Korean

Kwonsooksoo

Kwonsooksoo is a michellin star Korean restaurant where you can experience a thorough course meal of traditional korean food. Highly recommend for those who want to experience luxurious korean dinning experience.
Onjium

Onjium is a restaurant run by the professors from the institute of traditional Korean culture. The full course meal are preoared by the professors, who actually study Korean dishes. The quality of the meal is exceptional. The view of Gyeongbok palace, which is Korea's main palace, also plays a important role to the fine dinning experience.

Samwon Garden

Samwon Garden is one of the best korean bbq restaurants in Seoul. The restaurant resembles traditional korean hanok (houses), and is surrounded by beautiful garden with waterfalls. You must try their marinated beef and traditional nutritious rice.

Italian

Mongone

Mongone is a fairly new Italian restaurant that is extremely popular in Seoul. They are known for their uni pasta and vongole pasta. The uni pasta form Mongone is the best uni pasta that I have had in my life. Their pasta is perfectly cooked to the point where you can feel the chewy-ness of the pasta. Moreover, the uni perfectly blends into the pasta. As you put the pasta into your mouth, you would experience it melting in your mouth. Their vongole is also extremely delicious as well. If you are a vegetarian, I highly recommend their truffle risotto, where you can smell and taste the delicious truffle.

Terra 13

If you want traditional Italian food, I highly recommend Terra 13, located in the Cheongdam area. You can experience authentic Italian cuisine in the restaraunt. The must-try dishes include paccheri truffle cream pasta and Diabolo pizza. Note that their dishes are slightly salty because of their authentic Italian style of cooking. If you are someone, who eats blandly, make sure to let your waiter know!

Chinese

JS Garden

JS Garden is a popular Chinese restaurant that serves a la carte menu and course menu. It is a relatively high-end restaurant with amazing selections of Chinese food. They are known for their Peking duck, chili fried chicken wings, Jjajangmyeon, and Jjampong. If you want to order Peking duck, you have to call beforehand to reserve your duck! Moreover, the noodles they use for their noddle menu is unique in a way that they are sliced with the knife-cut technique!

Mong Jung Heon

If you are looking for an authentic dimsum, Mong Jung Heon is a place for you. The chefs at Mong Jung Heon, carefully make each dimsum piece. The dumpling wrappers are so thin that you can look through them and see the ingredients inside. The dimsums are so chewy and delicious that you will end up finishing multiple plates. They also provide wide selections of other Chinese dishes you can enjoy. Make sure to check this place out dimsum lovers.

Japanese

1. Sushi Sunsoo

For those who are looking for traditional omakase-style Japanese restaurants, Sushi Sunsoo is the go-to place for you. Sushi Sunsoo is one of the most renowned Japanese restaurants in Seoul. The chef uses fresh ingredients to cook delicious sushi. The quality of the overall omakase experience is uncomparably good for most places. You will get to devour each and every omakase piece in the restaurant.

Kappo Akii

Traditional “Kappo,” which refers to traditional Japanese cuisine using fresh ingredients, has been extremely famous in Korea lately. One of the most popular of those Kappo style restaurants is Kappo Aki. Kappo Aki is a perfect place for people who are looking to drink while eating deliciously cooked Japanese sushi and other dishes. Kappo Aki is known for its mackerel sushi, sukiyaki, and yakitoris. Make sure to check out this place if you are looking for delicious izakaya.
