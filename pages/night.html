I. Euljiro Nogari Alley

If you are looking for the classic k-drama street tent drinking, Nograi Alley is the perfect street to explore. Euljiro Nogari Alley is full of tent drinking places with various anjus (korean side dishes for drinking) and different types of soju. As you enter the alley, you will feel like you have become the main character of k dramas. The anjus are ranging from seafood soups to kimchi pancakes, will fill up your hungry belly. You should definitely check this alley out for a complete Korean experience!

II. SungSu Dong

Seongsu Dong is the Williamsburg of New York, characterized by its vibrant atmosphere and burgeoning community of Korean designer boutiques, charming cafes, and a diverse array of restaurants offering cuisine from around the world. Enjoy the mesmerizing Dior Flagship Store in Seong-su!

III. Cheongdam dong

Want to experience a more classy and special night life? Check out the luxurious hidden speakeasy bars in Chungdam area. Chungdam area is a known go-to place for those who want the most luxurious experience. The area is full of high-end restaurants and bars. Amongst luxury brands’ flagship stores and fine dining restaurants, L’Chamber is a hidden speakeasy bar located in the area. The entrance is a bookshelf and you have to press a specific book to enter the bar. Searching through different books to open the entrance, would be a fun challenge for you. Moreover, the bartender of the bar is a renown bartender who won several competitions. Make sure to try one and only “Chamber story,” which is a unique cocktail you can only try in L-Chamber.
